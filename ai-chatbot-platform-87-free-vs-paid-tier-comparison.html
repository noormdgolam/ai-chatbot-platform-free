AI Chatbot Platform 87 Free vs Paid Tier Comparison

Published: July 15, 2026 • Written by Editorial Team

Artificial intelligence (AI) chatbot platforms have become an essential tool for businesses looking to enhance customer experience and streamline operations. One such platform is Platform 87, which offers a range of features and tools to create AI-powered chatbots. In this comparison, we will delve into the differences between the free and paid tiers of Platform 87, helping you decide which plan best suits your needs.

Free Tier

The free tier of Platform 87 is ideal for small businesses or individuals who want to dip their toes into AI-powered chatbots. Here are the key features and limitations of the free tier:

  • Limited to 1 chatbot instance
  • 5,000 conversations per month (approximately 166 conversations per day)
  • Basic integrations with popular platforms like WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, and Telegram
  • No custom entity recognition or entity analytics
  • No support for custom intents and entities
  • No ability to create custom conversation flows or scenarios

The free tier is suitable for small-scale testing or low-traffic websites. If your business grows, you may find the limitations of the free tier restrictive.

Paid Tier

The paid tier of Platform 87 offers a range of advanced features and increased conversation limits, making it suitable for larger businesses or enterprises. Here are the key features and benefits of the paid tier:

  • Unlimited chatbot instances
  • 50,000 conversations per month (approximately 1,667 conversations per day)
  • Advanced integrations with popular platforms like WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, and Telegram
  • Custom entity recognition and entity analytics
  • Support for custom intents and entities
  • Ability to create custom conversation flows and scenarios
  • Priority support for technical issues

The paid tier is designed for scalability and offers flexibility for large businesses or enterprises. With increased conversation limits and advanced features, you can handle more traffic and create complex chatbots to meet your customers' needs.

Pricing

The pricing of Platform 87 is flexible and scalable, allowing you to choose a plan that matches your business needs. Here are the pricing details:

  • Free tier: Free (limited features)
  • Paid tier: Custom pricing (dependent on conversation limits and feature requirements)

The custom pricing model allows you to pay only for the features and conversation limits your business requires, making it an attractive option for large businesses or enterprises.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the free and paid tiers of Platform 87 offer distinct features and benefits. The free tier is ideal for small businesses or individuals looking to test AI-powered chatbots, while the paid tier is suitable for larger businesses or enterprises with high traffic and complex chatbots. By understanding the differences between the free and paid tiers, you can make an informed decision about which plan best suits your business needs.

Recommendation

We recommend the paid tier of Platform 87 for businesses with high traffic or large customer bases. The advanced features and increased conversation limits make it suitable for scalability and complexity, ultimately enhancing customer experience and driving business growth.
