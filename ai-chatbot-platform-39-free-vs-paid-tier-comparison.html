AI Chatbot Platform 39 Free vs Paid Tier Comparison

Published: July 15, 2026 • Written by Editorial Team

When considering the integration of AI chatbots into your business, one crucial decision you'll face is selecting a chatbot platform. There are numerous options available, with a range of free and paid tiers. In this article, we'll provide a comprehensive comparison of AI chatbot platform 39's free versus paid tier, highlighting key differences and benefits.

In the free tier, chatbot platform 39 offers a limited yet effective set of features. This tier supports the creation and deployment of basic chatbots that can engage with users in a conversational manner. The features include:

  • Conversational Flow: Users can create a basic conversational flow using the drag-and-drop interface.
  • Pre-built Templates: 39's chatbot platform offers pre-built templates for common use cases such as customer support and lead generation.
  • Integration with Third-party Services: Users can integrate their chatbot with third-party services like email marketing tools and CRM systems.
  • Basic Analytics: Free tier users have access to basic analytics that provide insights into chatbot performance and user engagement.

However, the free tier has its limitations. It's designed for small-scale use cases and is not suitable for complex or large-scale business applications. Users cannot:

  • Use Custom Domains: Chatbots created on the free tier cannot be deployed on custom domains.
  • Integrate with Advanced Services: Free tier users cannot integrate their chatbot with advanced services like AI-powered sentiment analysis or natural language processing.
  • Access Advanced Analytics: Basic analytics on the free tier are limited, and users cannot access advanced features like A/B testing or segmentation.
  • Enjoy 24/7 Support: Chatbot platform 39 offers limited support to free tier users, and they may not get assistance around the clock.

On the other hand, the paid tier offers more comprehensive features and benefits. With the paid tier, users can:

  • Use Custom Domains: Businesses can deploy their chatbots on custom domains, providing a professional and branded experience for users.
  • Integrate with Advanced Services: Paid tier users can integrate their chatbot with advanced services like AI-powered sentiment analysis or natural language processing, providing more sophisticated capabilities.
  • Access Advanced Analytics: Advanced analytics allow businesses to gain deeper insights into chatbot performance, user behavior, and conversion rates.
  • Enjoy 24/7 Support: Paid tier users receive priority support from chatbot platform 39, ensuring they get assistance around the clock.

Additionally, paid tier users get access to features like:

  • Bulk Message Sending: Users can send bulk messages to users and manage their conversations more effectively.
  • Conditional Logic: Paid tier users can create conditional logic to handle complex conversations and provide personalized responses.
  • API Access: Businesses can access the chatbot platform's API to build custom integrations and automate workflows.

In conclusion, AI chatbot platform 39's free tier is suitable for small-scale use cases or prototyping, while the paid tier offers more comprehensive features and benefits for businesses looking to invest in customer engagement and support.
