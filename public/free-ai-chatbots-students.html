Performance & Accuracy

In our benchmark testing for free AI chatbots for students, we evaluated logic, reasoning, and creative output. Hallucinations (AI confidently stating incorrect facts) are still a concern across the board, though paid tiers with real-time web browsing mitigate this significantly.
