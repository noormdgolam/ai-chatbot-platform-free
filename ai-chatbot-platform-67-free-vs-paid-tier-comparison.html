AI Chatbot Platform 67 Free vs Paid Tier Comparison

Published: July 15, 2026 • Written by Editorial Team

As the demand for AI-powered conversational interfaces continues to grow, organizations are turning to AI chatbot platforms to create personalized and efficient customer experiences. AI Chatbot Platform 67 is one such solution that offers both free and paid tiers to cater to various business needs. In this section, we will delve into a comprehensive comparison of the free and paid tiers of AI Chatbot Platform 67.

Overview

AI Chatbot Platform 67 is a cloud-based platform that enables businesses to design, develop, and deploy conversational AI chatbots to engage with customers across various channels, including messaging apps, websites, and mobile apps. The platform's AI engine can handle a wide range of tasks, from answering frequently asked questions (FAQs) to resolving complex issues.

Free Tier

The free tier of AI Chatbot Platform 67 is ideal for small businesses or startups that want to test the waters without committing to a paid subscription. The free tier comes with a range of limitations, including:

  • Conversational sessions: 500 per month (reset at the start of each month)
  • AI model training: Limited to 1,000 intents and entities
  • Data storage: 500MB (can be upgraded or exceeded, incurring additional costs)
  • Customer support: Limited to 10 hours of basic support per quarter

Paid Tier

The paid tier of AI Chatbot Platform 67 offers advanced features and a higher level of support to meet the needs of larger businesses. The paid tier comes with the following benefits:

  • Conversational sessions: No limit (suitable for businesses with high customer engagement)
  • AI model training: Unlimited intents and entities (allowing for more sophisticated models)
  • Data storage: 5GB (can be upgraded or exceeded, incurring additional costs)
  • Customer support: 24/7 access to expert support, including phone, email, and chat support
  • Advanced analytics: Access to in-depth analytics and reporting to track business performance

Pricing

The pricing of AI Chatbot Platform 67 can be broken down into two main plans: the Basic plan and the Advanced plan.

  • Basic plan: $99/month (pay as you go)
  • Advanced plan: Custom pricing (based on business-specific requirements)

It is essential to note that prices may be subject to change and may not include any additional services or features. We recommend verifying the pricing with the AI Chatbot Platform 67 vendor.

Conclusion

AI Chatbot Platform 67 offers a solid solution for businesses looking to enhance their customer experience through conversational AI. While the free tier is a great starting point, the paid tier offers a range of advanced features and higher level of support suitable for larger businesses. By weighing the pros and cons of each tier, organizations can make an informed decision that aligns with their specific needs and goals.
