AI Chatbot Platform 85 Free vs Paid Tier Comparison

Published: July 15, 2026 • Written by Editorial Team

When it comes to selecting an AI chatbot platform, one of the primary decisions you'll need to make is whether to opt for the free tier or the paid tier. While the free tier can be a great way to get started and test the waters, it often comes with significant limitations, which can hinder the full potential of your chatbot. In contrast, the paid tier offers a range of advanced features, greater flexibility, and dedicated support, making it an attractive option for businesses looking to integrate AI-powered chatbots into their customer engagement strategy.

To understand the key differences between the free and paid tiers, let's take a closer look at AI Chatbot Platform 85's offerings.

Free Tier: The free tier of AI Chatbot Platform 85 is designed to be a low-impact, low-risk option for businesses looking to dip their toes into AI-powered chatbots. With this tier, you'll get access to basic features, such as:

  • Pre-built chatbot templates: Get started quickly with pre-designed chatbot templates, covering common scenarios like customer support, order tracking, and FAQ.
  • Simple messaging APIs: Send and receive messages with users through various messaging channels, such as WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, and SMS.
  • Basic analytics: Track key metrics, such as conversation volume, user engagement, and chat success rate.
  • Limited customizations: Make some customizations to the chatbot's interface and behavior, but with limited flexibility.

While the free tier is a great way to get started, it has its limitations. For instance, you'll face limits on the number of users, conversations, and messages you can send and receive within a given time frame. Additionally, customizations are limited, and you may not have access to advanced features like sentiment analysis, entity recognition, or integrations with third-party services.

Paid Tier: The paid tier of AI Chatbot Platform 85 offers significantly advanced features, greater flexibility, and dedicated support, making it a more attractive option for businesses looking to scale their chatbot integration. Some of the key benefits of the paid tier include:

  • Advanced chatbot templates: Get access to more sophisticated pre-built chatbot templates, covering specialized scenarios like customer service automation, sales and marketing, and HR support.
  • Advanced messaging APIs: Integrate with a wider range of messaging channels, including web, iOS, Android, and more.
  • In-depth analytics: Get access to more detailed analytics, including conversation analytics, sentiment analytics, and user segmentation.
  • Dedicated support: Enjoy priority support from the AI Chatbot Platform 85 team, including customized onboarding and ongoing support.
  • Extensive customizations: Make extensive customizations to the chatbot's interface and behavior, including custom integrations with third-party services.

In addition to these features, the paid tier also removes the limitations of the free tier, allowing you to scale your chatbot integration to meet the needs of your business.

When choosing between the free and paid tiers of AI Chatbot Platform 85, consider the following factors:

  • Business size and scope: If you have a small team or are still testing the waters, the free tier may be sufficient. However, if you're looking to deploy chatbots at scale, the paid tier is a more suitable option.
  • Feature requirements: If you need advanced features like sentiment analysis or custom integrations, the paid tier is the better choice.
  • Support needs: If you require dedicated support, the paid tier is the better option.

Ultimately, selecting the right tier for your AI chatbot platform depends on your business needs and goals. By weighing the pros and cons of each tier, you'll be able to make an informed decision that meets your needs and sets you up for success with AI-powered chatbots.
